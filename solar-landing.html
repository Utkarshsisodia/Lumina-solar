Common questions.

What if my roof needs replacing soon?

We recommend replacing your roof before installing solar if it's over 10 years old. We work with trusted local roofing partners and can roll the cost into your solar financing package.

Do the panels work during a power outage?

Standard grid-tied systems automatically shut off during an outage for safety. However, if you add our Lumina Home Battery, your home will seamlessly switch to backup power instantly.

How long does the installation take?

The physical installation takes just 1-2 days. The complete process, including permits, custom design, and utility connection, usually takes 4-6 weeks.
