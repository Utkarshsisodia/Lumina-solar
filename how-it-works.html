The science of savings.

Understanding how sunlight becomes reliable, clean electricity for your home doesn't require a degree in engineering. Here is how your system works.

Sunlight to Power.

Every component in our system is designed to maximize efficiency and integrate seamlessly into your home's existing electrical infrastructure.

When sunlight hits the monocrystalline silicon cells in your panels, it knocks electrons loose, creating a flow of direct current (DC) electricity. This clean energy is instantly converted and routed to where your home needs it most.

1. Solar Panels (DC Power)

High-efficiency roof panels absorb photons and generate Direct Current electrical power.

2. Smart Inverter

Converts the raw DC power into safe, usable Alternating Current (AC) for your home's appliances.

3. Home Electrical Panel

The AC power flows into your breaker box, powering your lights, AC, and devices first.

4. Net Metering

Any excess energy your home doesn't use is sent back to the grid, earning you credits from your utility.
